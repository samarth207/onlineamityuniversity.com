Frequently Asked Questions

What is Amity University Online?
Amity University Online is India's first UGC-approved online university offering globally recognized online undergraduate, postgraduate, and certificate programs. Designed for working professionals and students, the platform provides the flexibility to advance education and careers without compromising existing commitments.
What are the admission requirements?
Admission requirements vary by program: For undergraduate programs: Completion of 10+2. For postgraduate programs: A bachelor's degree is required. Academic certificates and a valid government-issued ID. Some programs may require additional eligibility tests.
When are admissions open?
Admissions are conducted twice annually. Updates and deadlines are posted on the official website.
How do I confirm my admission?
After completing the enrollment process, students receive a confirmation email with login credentials for the E-Campus portal, granting access to learning materials and resources.
Can I cancel my admission?
Yes, admission cancellations are reviewed individually. Refunds, if applicable, are processed as per university policies. The admissions team can assist with cancellation requests.
What documents are required for admission?
For a smooth admission process, the following documents are required: For Domestic Students: Academic certificates from 10th, 12th, and graduation. Consolidated marksheets for graduation. A valid government-issued ID such as Aadhar, PAN, or Passport. For International Students: O Level and A Level certificates or equivalent qualifications. Bachelor's degree certificate with consolidated marksheets. Passport copy (mandatory). Once these documents are ready, our admissions team will guide you through the process.
Can the online MBA program be completed while working full-time?
Yes, with online MBA programs, candidates can pursue part-time or full-time jobs. This is because online MBAs do not demand students to attend regular and in-person classes. They allow students the flexibility to balance their studies with their work and family.
How do I access my course?
Once enrolled, students receive login credentials for the student portal. This platform provides access to all learning resources, including live lectures, recorded sessions, e-books, and assignments. AI-Professor AMI is also available 24/7 to assist with academic queries. The learning platform also provides various skill enhancement courses to enhance learners' employability skills.
What study resources are available?
Students have access to: Live and recorded lectures by experienced Indian & global faculty and industry experts. E-books, physical study materials, and case studies. Collaborative activities and webinars.
How are classes conducted?
Classes are delivered through live interactive sessions, recorded lectures, and forums for discussions. This hybrid model ensures a flexible yet engaging learning experience. Students can also participate in beSocial for additional collaborative and peer-based learning opportunities.
How are exams conducted?
Exams are conducted in a secure online proctored environment on the student portal. The system uses advanced technology to maintain academic integrity. Assessments also include quizzes, assignments, and hands-on projects, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation. Faculty and AI-Professor AMI are available to support students and help them prepare for exams better.
Where can I find the academic calendar?
The academic calendar is accessible on the student portal and provides details on assignment deadlines, examination schedules, and other milestones. The student community portal, beSocial, also updates students with reminders about key academic events.
How can I interact with faculty?
Faculty interaction is facilitated through live Q&A sessions, offline meet-ups, and discussion forums. AI-Professor AMI provides additional support for instant query resolution.
Are additional courses or certifications offered?
Yes, over 75 open learning courses and free certifications in areas like Advanced Excel, Leadership, and Strategic HR are available to enhance employability. Collaborative learning opportunities on beSocial further enrich the experience.
What is the admission process?
The admission process is simple: Select your program and complete the online application form. Upload the required documents and pay the registration fee. Upon acceptance, confirm your enrollment by making the program fee payment. The admissions team and AI-Professor AMI are available to assist at every step.
