About Amity Online

Empowering Lives Through Accessible, Quality Education

Our Story

Amity Online University was founded with a singular mission: to democratize access to world-class education. We believe that quality education should not be limited by geography, time constraints, or life circumstances. Through innovative online learning platforms and industry-aligned curriculum, we've empowered over 2 lakh professionals to advance their careers while balancing work and personal commitments.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and industry-relevant online education that empowers learners to achieve their professional and personal goals while maintaining the highest academic standards.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted online university, recognized for innovation, academic excellence, and transforming lives through flexible, learner-centric education.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, Accessibility, Student Success, Continuous Improvement, and Social Responsibility guide everything we do.
