Amity University Online Scholarships

At Amity University Online, we believe quality education should be accessible to all - regardless of geographical or financial constraints. That's why we offer a wide range of scholarships to support students in pursuing their academic dreams. Our scholarship programs include the Sports Scholarship (CHAMPS), Defence Scholarship, Divyaang Scholarship, Merit-based, and Amity Alumni Scholarship. These initiatives and opportunities are tailored to reward talent, dedication, and leadership across disciplines.

We are committed to empowering students with internationally recognized degrees, expert faculty mentorship, and industry-aligned programs. Through our scholarships, we provide financial assistance to make higher education more accessible and affordable.

Students aspiring to pursue Bachelor's and Master's degrees like BBA, MBA, MCA, BCA, MA, or BA with us are provided the financial assistance they need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our scholarship programs

Who is eligible for the Merit-based Scholarship and how can I apply?

Students who have secured a minimum of 85% aggregate in their previous qualification are eligible for up to 20% reduction on full program fee.

To apply, simply fill in your details in the scholarship form and upload your academic transcripts. The university will verify the documents before approval.

What is the CHAMPS Sports Scholarship and what are the eligibility requirements?

The CHAMPS Scholarship is offered to students who have participated in state, national, or international level sports. Scholarships range from 30% to 100% fee reduction, depending on the level of achievement and valid affiliation.

To apply, you must submit:
- Certificates of participation & achievement
- Valid affiliation certificate from a recognized sports federation

Who is eligible for the Defence Personnel Scholarship and how do I apply?

Individuals who have served/serving at the Defence Forces, Para Military forces or Police Services are eligible for a 20% fee reduction.

To apply, submit:
- Fee concession form for Defence Forces, Para Military & Police Forces to be filled
- A service certificate verified by the Commanding Officer /Zila Sainik Board / Local Station Commander

*Scholarship approval is subject to document verification.*

What disabilities are covered under the Divyaang (Persons with Special Needs) Scholarship?

The Divyaang Scholarship is available for students with disabilities recognized under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPWD) Act, 2016.

The Act includes 6 disability categories, which cover 21 sub-categories such as:-
Physical Disability- Locomotor disability- Leprosy Cured Person- Cerebral Palsy- Dwarfism- Muscular Dystrophy- Blindness- Low-Vision- Deaf & Hard of Hearing- Speech & Language Disability- Intellectual disability- Specific Learning Disability- Autism Spectrum Disorder- Mental behaviour- Mental Illness- Disability caused due to Chronic Neurological Conditions- Multiple Sclerosis- Parkinson's Disease- Disability caused due to Blood disorder- Haemophilia- Thalassemia- Sickle Cell Disease- Multiple Disabilities- Deaf-Blindness

To apply, students must submit a valid government-issued disability certificate as per the RPWD Act norms with disability of 40% and above.

What are the eligibility criteria for the Amity Alumni Scholarship?

The Amity Alumni Scholarship is available to individuals who have previously completed a program with Amity University. Eligible alumni can avail a 20% fee reduction on their course fees by providing required documents.

Can I avail more than one scholarship or bundle offer at the same time?

No, students are eligible for only one scholarship or offer at a time.

Are there any fee reductions for full or annual fee payments?

Yes, Amity University Online offers:
- 12% discount for full program fee payment (UG programs)
- 8% discount for full program fee payment (PG programs)
- 5% discount for annual fee payment (UG & PG programs )

These discounts are automatically applied when you choose the respective payment option during enrollment.
